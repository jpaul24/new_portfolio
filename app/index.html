Jaunt

Jaunt is a Ruby on Rails app optimised for mobile. It was the final project at Le Wagon where I worked as part of a wonderful team of 4

I pitched the idea and people chose to work on it with me so I took on a collaborative project management role to bring the vision to life.

Described best as Tinder for travelling, the user enters a city they're visiting and how long they're are going for and the app will offer a range of activities to swipe through and then will auto-populate your calendar for your time there.

The app includes: devise (logins); pundit (authentication); geocoding and maps (mapbox); javascript for swipe (swing.js); AJAX; cloudinary for image upload.

Let It Grow

A Ruby on Rails app built with the same team as Jaunt, to understand workflow and building as part of a team. It is a market place app based on Air BnB.

It is a site on which a user can rent or rent out a garden, be it for a party or a casual bit of gardening.

The app includes: devise (logins); geocoding and maps (mapbox); AJAX (star rating)

Real Good Fitness

Static website built for a local Personal Trainer to improve their online presence and build more business from the area.

Wireframed before building using Figma to understand the importance of having a design to follow during development. Used front end design and the CSS and HTML skills that I have developed strong skills in.
